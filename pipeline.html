Audio Pipeline

How frames move between your app and Discord — outbound encode and inbound decode.

⬆

Outbound — App → Discord

  1. 1 Float32 input at 48kHz stereo
  2. 2 Clamp to [−1, 1]
  3. 3 Convert to Int16 (scaled by ±32767/32768)
  4. 4 Accumulate into 960-sample (20ms) frames
  5. 5 Pipe to prism Opus encoder
  6. 6 Feed Opus stream to AudioPlayer
  7. 7 AudioPlayer publishes to Discord voice connection
⬇

Inbound — Discord → App

  1. 1 VoiceReceiver subscribes to user Opus stream
  2. 2 prism Opus decoder → Int16 buffer
  3. 3 Convert Int16 → Float32 (divide by 32768)
  4. 4 Emit via callback at 48kHz stereo

Connection Recovery

Listens for the Disconnected voice state and calls rejoin() immediately. Complements any host-app reconnect timer. Voice join sends an op-4 leave first to clear stale sessions before retry.
